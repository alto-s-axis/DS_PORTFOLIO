Software Services

Automation

This intelligent automation revolutionizes how students learn, making education more efficient and tailored to individual strengths.

08

Security

DedSec Security Advancement by Dedraj Studio offers a next-level approach to campus safety. Powered by AI and real-time monitoring, DedSec ensures 24/7 surveillance of school premises, detecting and responding to security threats instantly. With features like facial recognition, access control, and smart alert systems, DedSec provides a robust, scalable security framework that protects students, staff, and assets. This cutting-edge solution not only enhances physical security but also integrates seamlessly with other campus management systems, offering a comprehensive, proactive approach to safety and peace of mind.

09

Transport & GPS

Dedraj Studios Fleet Management solution ensures efficient and secure transportation for schools. By integrating GPS tracking, real-time monitoring, and route optimization, we help streamline vehicle operations, improve safety, and reduce costs. Our system provides live updates on vehicle location, student pick-up and drop-off times, and ensures that each journey is monitored for timely, safe, and efficient transportation. With Dedrajs Fleet Management, schools can ensure a reliable, smooth transportation experience for both students and staff while minimizing operational overhead.
